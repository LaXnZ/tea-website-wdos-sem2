SENOK TEA CEYLON PVT LTD

<5br> Senok Tea (Pvt) Ltd was established in 1989 and is a part of the prestigious Senok Group which is headquartered in Colombo, Sri Lanka. The Company was formed with the vision of providing Pure Ceylon Tea incorporating high quality products and tea services to the growing international tea market. The Company gained vast popularity for its exceptional products and services both in the local and international tea markets, especially Europe, Asia and the Far East.

Senok Tea has been globally acclaimed for its remarkably successful business marketing performance and was awarded the prestigious Presidential Certificate of Merit. The company takes great pride in this achievement and is honored to partner with the steady influx of clients who value its significance. The company’s main area of expertise is exporting value added teas, unique tea bags, and specially packaged tea per year, The company exports over 1.5 million kilograms of tea and also produces over 600 million tea bags for the export market annually.
